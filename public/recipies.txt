### 1. Cabbage and Pepper Stir-Fry

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cabbage
- 1 red pepper
- 1 yellow pepper
- 1 orange pepper
- 1 green onion
- 1 mushroom
- Olive oil (if available)
- Salt and pepper (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. Slice the cabbage into thin strips and chop the peppers into bite-sized pieces.
2. Finely chop the green onion and slice the mushroom.
3. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Add the green onion and mushroom, sautéing for about 2 minutes until softened.
5. Add the cabbage and peppers to the skillet, stirring frequently. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve warm as a side dish or over rice.

### 2. Cheesy Cabbage and Egg Bake

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cabbage
- 1 cheese
- 4 eggs
- 1 green onion
- Salt and pepper (if available)
- Olive oil or butter (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Chop the cabbage into small pieces and finely chop the green onion.
3. In a skillet, heat a little olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add the cabbage and green onion, cooking until the cabbage is wilted (about 5-7 minutes).
4. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, salt, and pepper. Stir in the cooked cabbage mixture and grated cheese.
5. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the top is golden.
6. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.

### 3. Raspberry and Cucumber Salad

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cucumbers
- 1 cup raspberries
- 1 lettuce
- 1 green onion
- Olive oil (if available)
- Vinegar (if available)
- Salt and pepper (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. Slice the cucumbers into thin rounds and chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
2. In a large bowl, combine the cucumbers, raspberries, and lettuce.
3. Finely chop the green onion and add it to the salad.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss gently to combine.
5. Serve immediately as a refreshing side salad.
